Vada

About the dish:

Vada is an indian snack that everyone can enjoy. It's a quintessential part of South Indian breakfast. A perfect match to steamed idli. It is a very popular in all the Southern Indian states. Telugus call it as garelu. You can have it with sambar and rasam which are both lentil stews. You can have it dry too with some coconut , tomato pachdi or ginger chutney.

Ingredients

  • Urad dal/ split black gram lentils
  • Onion
  • Green chili
  • Coriander leaves/curry leaves
  • Black peppers
  • Rice flour optional
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Soak the urad dal in water for 4 hours/overnight. Always better to throw the water away and then grind with minimal fresh water in a wet grinder or mixer grinder.
  2. Add other ingredients to the dal. You can also add some curry leaves to medhu vadai.
  3. Mix all ingredients.
  4. Take a spoonful and make a ring of the batter, let it cook in medium hot oil.
  5. Take them out of the oil once cooked/golden brown
  6. Serve medu vada hot with coconut chutney or tomato pachadi.
